// 01 — Overview

RARE BY
DESIGN

Eden Exotics is a boutique exotic vehicle curator serving an ultra-high-net-worth clientele. When they came to us, they had an exceptional inventory and a passion for the extraordinary — but a brand that wasn't communicating that at all.

We rebuilt their brand from the ground up with one guiding principle: rarity deserves reverence. Every decision — from the weight of the typeface to the temperature of the photography — was designed to signal exclusivity without explicitly stating it. The result speaks to people who already know what true luxury means.

// 02 — Art Direction

THE ART OF THE EXTRAORDINARY

Art direction for Eden Exotics demanded a filmic quality — deep shadows, selective light, and compositions that treated each vehicle as sculpture. We developed a full photography and visual direction guide for their in-house team to maintain consistency.

  • Photography direction standards
  • Dark editorial visual language
  • Vehicle showcase page templates
  • Social content system
