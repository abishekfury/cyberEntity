// 01 — Overview

LESS IS
EVERYTHING

OSO is a lifestyle brand built on a deceptively simple premise: that the most powerful creative statement is knowing what to leave out. When they approached us, they had a clear product vision but needed a brand framework that could hold that philosophy without over-explaining it.

We built an identity defined by restraint. A wordmark reduced to pure geometry. A color system of four. A grid structure that treats silence as a design element. The result is a brand identity that, like the best minimal design, creates more questions than it answers — and keeps people looking.

// 02 — Apparel & Product

WORN CONVICTION

Every apparel application was treated as a piece of ambient communication — a billboard worn by the customer. The identity system was optimized for placement, scale, and context across garments, accessories, and packaging.
