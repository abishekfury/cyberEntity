// 01 — Overview

ROOTED IN
NATURE

Wild Flower is a wellness brand that draws identity from the raw, untamed beauty of botanicals. The brief called for a visual world that was simultaneously delicate and bold — something that could live comfortably on a retail shelf while commanding attention online.

We developed a brand universe rooted in natural texture, earthy palettes pushed to modern contrast levels, and a typographic system that let the product photography breathe while anchoring every layout with quiet confidence.
